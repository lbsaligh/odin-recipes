Spaghetti Carbonara

Description:

An italian classic made from only a few ingredients is sure to blow your guests away when done correctly! The spaghetti carbonara tastes delicious regardless of the season or occassin.

Ingredients:

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • salt and pepper for taste

Steps:

  1. Bring a large saucepan to the boil and add the spaghetti and cook till al dente
  2. Bring the pecorino cheese, parmesan and eggs together to create a carbonara mix
  3. Fry the pancetta with the garlic with the butter
  4. Once the pancetta is fried off and golden in colour, turn the heat down to low
  5. Add the spaghetti to the pan and keep some of the pasta water
  6. Take the pan off the heat and quickly add the carbonara mix and stir
  7. Add some pasta water to the pan to loosen the mix and evenly coat the pasta
  8. You're now done! Add the spaghetti to the plate and sprinkle some parmesan cheese on top
