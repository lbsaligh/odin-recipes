Sausage Casserole

Description:

A sausage casserole. A classic. A winter warmer of ultimate proportions. Comfort at it's finest.

A recipe so warming and tasty for those cold, winter months. Where each bite feels like a bite from the high heavens.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium sticks celery, finely chopped
  • 1 yellow pepper, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 6 cooking chorizo sausages (about 400g)
  • 6 pork sausages (about 400g)
  • 3 fat garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 ½ tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 125ml white wine
  • 2 x 400g cans cherry tomatoes or chopped tomatoes
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 x 400g can aduki beans, drained and rinsed

Step-by-step guide:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan: and add the onion and other vegetables.
  2. Fry chorizo and pork sausages: and stir in the garlic and spices.
  3. Add the cans of tomatoes: and simmer for 40 minutes until reduced then serve.
