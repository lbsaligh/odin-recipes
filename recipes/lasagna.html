Lasagna

Description:

Is there anything more comforting than a nice slab of lasagna? With intricate layers of tasty goodness full of beef and tomato, what is not to like about this italian invention?

It's hard not to love this classic dish and we have perfected this recipe in such a way that it will be a show stopper whenver you cook it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 500g spaghetti sauce (marinara)
  • 500g cottage cheese
  • 300g mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 tablespoon (tbsp) dried parsley
  • Himalayan salt
  • 5 lasagna sheets
  • Cup of water
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 5 cloves garlic

Step-by-step guide:

  1. Make the lasagna sauce using the ground beef, vegetables, marinara sauce, tomato puree, garlic and herbs and let simmer for 20 mins until the liquid reduces and thickens.
  2. let the sauce simmer whilst grating the cheese and boil the lasagne sheets. Whilst the lasagna sheets are boiling, slice and grate the required amount of mozzarella, ricotta cheese and parmesan.
  3. Time to assemble! In a baking tray, add a layer of meat sauce, a lasagna sheet, more sauce and then the cheesy goodness! Keep repeating this process for all lasagna sheets and then on the final layer, add the meat sauce and finish with the rest of the cheese.
  4. Let's bake! Finally, add the tray of lasagna goodness to a pre-heated oven at fan 180 degrees celsius and let that bake for 20 mins or until the cheese has melted and browned a little bit.
